Our Mission

Food is a common necessaity for humans to survive Even though
there are lots of food produced we waste a lot of it ,thus our
goal is to reduce wastage and help economically weaker .

To feed people

About 829 million people are malnourished
in world. We wish to feed nutritious food
to as many people as we can.

To Enrich Communities

We are creating network amoung local community.
One of our objective is to collect excess food
from a particular community and deliver to the needen.

Maintaining Hygine

Providing nutritious healthy food to the needed is
our priority . We don't make any exception when it
to quality of food.
